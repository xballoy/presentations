# Key takeaways

## Five demos, one approach

1. **Analyze**: the AI explores, we validate the findings
2. **Plan**: the AI proposes a plan, we steer it
3. **Decide**: the AI compares the alternatives, we choose
4. **Simplify**: the AI spots unnecessary dependencies, we measure the effort
5. **Automate**: the AI writes the codemod, we validate the diff

<br />

## Where to start on your projects

- **Start small**: code analysis (low risk)
- **Develop your critical thinking**: question everything
- **Expand progressively**: migration → refactoring → automation
- **Keep control**: AI proposes, you decide
